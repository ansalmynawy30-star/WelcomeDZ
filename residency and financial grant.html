Study in Algeria – Residency & Financial Support

This page helps international students obtain residence
permits and financial aid.


Residency card :

documents required:
1. Passport (3 copies).
2. Birth Certificate (3 copies).
3. Consular Certificate (3 copies).
4. Registration Form (3 copies).
5. Original Residence Certificate.
6. Old Residence Permit.
7. Ministry License (3 copies).
8. Cash Receipt or Postal Bank Statement.
9. 12 Passport-Size Photos.
10. Personal Hygiene Test.
11. Financial Receipt.

Note: The value of the financial receipt is determined by the competent authorities (according to the length of stay).


Note: Submit these documents to the authorized security center in your area.


Note: A temporary residence permit will be issued for 3 months before granting official residence.


Financial Support :

Official documents required for students with non-Algerian secondary school certificates :

- Registration number (obtained from the student's embassy).

- Original high school transcript (certified by the Ministry of Higher Education and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs).

- Birth certificate (certified by the Ministry of Higher Education and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs).

- Recent passport-size photographs (3 copies).

- Medical certificate confirming the student is free from infectious diseases.

- Copy of a valid passport.
